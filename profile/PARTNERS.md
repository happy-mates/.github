# Our Partners

Happy Mates collaborates with educational institutions and organizations to provide comprehensive pathways for young people with autism and IT interests.

## Educational Partners

### AspIT
**Denmark's Leading IT Institution for Youth with Autism**

[AspIT](https://aspit.dk/) is our primary educational partner, providing foundational IT education for participants before they join our AI specialization program. AspIT specializes in IT education tailored for young people with autism, offering structured learning environments and comprehensive support systems.

**Partnership Focus:**
- Foundational 2-year IT education programs
- Student recruitment for AI specialization
- Curriculum alignment with industry needs
- Educational support and mentorship

---

### Chaplin
**Professional Development and Career Services**

[Chaplin](https://www.chaplin.as/) partners with Happy Mates to support career development and employment pathways for our participants.

**Partnership Focus:**
- Career counseling and guidance
- Job placement support
- Professional development resources
- Employer connections

---

### VUC Nordsjaelland (VUC NS)
**Flexible Higher Preparatory Education**

[VUC Nordsjaelland](https://www.vucns.dk/uddannelser/hf-fleksibel-uddannelse-der-aabner-doere/hf-in) offers flexible HF (Higher Preparatory Examination) programs that open doors to further education and career opportunities.

**Partnership Focus:**
- Alternative educational pathways
- Flexible learning options
- Academic preparation and support
- Transition support to IT education

---
